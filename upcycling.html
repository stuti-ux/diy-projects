Upcycled Mason Jars

Donâ€™t throw away old mason jars! Turn them into charming storage containers, planters, or candle holders with this simple project.

What You'll Need:

  • Old mason jars
  • Acrylic paint or chalk paint
  • Paintbrush
  • Twine, ribbon, or fabric
  • Optional: LED candles or small plants

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Clean the Jars: Wash and dry the jars thoroughly to remove any dirt or residue.
  2. Paint the Jars: Apply acrylic or chalk paint to the exterior of the jars. You may need multiple coats for full coverage.
  3. Add Decorations: Wrap twine, ribbon, or fabric around the neck of the jars for a rustic touch.
  4. Use Your Creations: Fill the jars with items like utensils, pens, plants, or LED candles for a functional and decorative piece.

Upcycled Tin Can Planters

Transform old tin cans into beautiful planters for your home or garden. This easy project adds a touch of greenery while reducing waste.

What You'll Need:

  • Empty tin cans
  • Spray paint or acrylic paint
  • Paintbrush
  • Soil and small plants
  • Optional: Nail and hammer for drainage holes

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Prep the Cans: Clean and dry the cans thoroughly. If desired, use a nail and hammer to add drainage holes to the bottom.
  2. Paint the Cans: Apply spray paint or acrylic paint to the exterior of the cans. Get creative with colors and patterns.
  3. Plant Your Greenery: Fill the cans with soil and add small plants or herbs. Water lightly and place in a sunny spot.
  4. Display: Use your upcycled planters indoors or outdoors to brighten up any space.
